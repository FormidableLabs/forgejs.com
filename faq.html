Frequently Asked Questions

How much JavaScript do I need to know already?

While there is no formal application process to attend any of the intensives, participants will be best served if they have a base level of experience with and understanding of the JavaScript language. This includes being able to fluently use, at a minimum:

  • Data types
  • Conditionals
  • Functions
  • Objects, properties & methods
  • Iterators
  • jQuery API for DOM manipulation

If you aren't already at this level or are not sure what some of the above terms mean, we would recommend waiting to attend a ForgeJS event and starting with some of these online resources:

  • A re-introduction to JavaScript
  • Codecademy
  • Code School

Can I register for all three workshops?

Yes, email us at info@forgejs.com if you'd like to attend all three workshops. The combined rates are $1550 for early bird registration and $2000 for regular registration.

What do I need to bring?

In addition to your own laptop with WiFi, we'll send you a list of suggested reading and tools you'll need to have configured on your machine prior to the start of the intensive.

I'm hungry, send help!

Coffee, a light breakfast and catered lunch will be provided on Saturday and Sunday during each weekend. In addition, the HUB is located in the International District of Seattle which has a fantastic range of dining options.

Where are the events held?

The HUB Seattle, 220 2nd Ave South, Seattle, WA 98104. The HUB is in the triangle-shaped building in between the two forks of 2nd Ave S. Entrance on the West side.

Parking? Public transit?

Paid parking is available in various nearby lots for roughly $15 per day. The HUB is two blocks away from the Pioneer Square station in the bus tunnel and is close to numerous surface bus lines.

Can I transfer my registration?

You can transfer your ticket to another person by contacting info@forgejs.com and letting us know who the registration should be transferred to. Your email must match the one you bought the ticket with.

Can I cancel my registration?

Need to cancel your registration? Notify us in writing at info@forgejs.com within 15 days of the start of your workshop for a refund less a $100 fee. Cancellation less than 15 days before the start of the workshop is non-refundable. Confirmed attendees who do not attend or cancel within 15 days are not eligable for a refund. In the event the training is cancelled, the liability of ForgeJS is limited to the return of paid registration fees.
